Becky worked in groups I managed as CTO and CEO at Global Solar Energy (GSE) in Tucson, Arizona. In particular, we worked together on the same team for many key projects during that time and I was able to observe her work style, ethics, and leadership qualities. I can highly recommend Becky for a leadership role in any project in which complex Data Management is a key element.
GSE was a startup company commercializing a unique and complex solar technology with many manufacturing steps. Large volumes of data were generated by the production tools. It was critical to the scale-up to collect, manipulate, and present the data to the Manufacturing and Process Engineering Teams so they could control and continuously improve production output. Becky assumed increasing responsibilities for developing and maintaining a custom Manufacturing Execution System (MES) during her tenure at GSE.
Becky was promoted into leadership positions because she was very successful at making the MES essential to all user groups. She used a methodical approach in multidisciplinary team meetings to poll participants on their department needs, and gain consensus on ranking subprojects within and across departments including Production, Engineering, Finance, Accounting, Product Development, and Sales. She helped them flesh out their specific requirements and thoroughly documented the process. She was universally respected for her work product quality and the rational, measured way in which it was produced. She developed exemplary leadership qualities to help her coordinate with all the parties involved. The MES was ultimately an extraordinary tool for accelerating GSE production capacity and quality.
I thoroughly enjoyed my time working with Becky and came to know her as a truly valuable asset to absolutely any team. She is extremely knowledgeable about all things IT, honest, dependable, and incredibly hard-working. Along with her undeniable talent, Becky was always been an absolute joy to work with. She is a true team player and ambitious, yet always managed to foster positive discussions and bring the best out of her reports and other employees.
Without a doubt, I confidently recommend Becky to join your Company in a similar, or even higher position in IT. As a dedicated and knowledgeable employee and an all-around great person, I know that she will be a beneficial addition to your organization.
